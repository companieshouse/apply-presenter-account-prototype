Companies House

Filing accounts by software only 

As part of our journey to modernise and digitise our filing routes, the way you file your annual accounts with Companies House is changing. 

From XX DATE, anyone filing company accounts will need to use software. You will not be able to file company accounts online or send paper accounts by post. 

Using software is a quick, efficient and secure way to file your company accounts. Moving to a single, cost-effective, sustainable and traceable way to file will also improve the quality of the data on the register. 

What you need to do 

You will need to apply for a Companies House online filing presenter account to file accounts for your own company, or on behalf of other companies: https://www.gov.uk/apply-for-presenter-account

If you’re a company director and you use an accountant to file your annual accounts, talk to them about software filing to make sure you’re compliant with the upcoming changes. 

Most companies can make the change now as software is already available.  There are many software providers who offer a range of accounting packages to prepare and file accounts.

 Find the right software package for your needs: https://www.gov.uk/company-filing-software.
